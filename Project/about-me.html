About Me

I am a college student and I started drawing in my free time during middle school. Over time, once I entered high school I was introduced into Japanese manga, bearing obvious similarities to comics here in the west, but with a distinct difference in content, targeting late-teenagers with fantasy or other action scenarios.
Eventually people noticed what I was drawing and started asking if I could do drawings of them in specific scenarios like fighting dragons or fighting Darth Vader, so once I got out of high school I figured it would be fun to continue doing the drawings for people, and this is where I ended up.
